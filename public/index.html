      Travel Restrictions Insights

      Planning any trip starts with checking travel restrictions and risk level for your destination. It includes information about self-quarantine needs, health certification requirement, airlines information and both internal and international restrictions.

      • Certificate Requirement

        COVID-19 negative certification might be required in some countries. In such case, passengers must provide negative COVID-19 test results before boarding. That's a crucial thing when planning your next trip.

      • Health Restrictions

        Passengers travelling to some countries may need to complete a health declaration form, answer questions related to the presence of symptoms which could be related to COVID-19 virus and more.

      • Transportation Limitations

        COVID-19 has affected transportation. You must be aware of the transportation limits in each destination you plan to visit.

      • Airlines Information

        What is the response of airlines to COVID-19 situation? The COVID-19 pandemic has led to a massive disruption to flight operations, with numerous airport restrictions.
